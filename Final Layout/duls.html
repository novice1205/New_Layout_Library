Delhi University Library System (DULS)


Delhi University Library System (DULS) with more than 37 libraries under its wing, the DULS is successful in reaching a larger academic community. With the subscription of up to 64 high quality electronic databases, which are made available to professors, students, and researchers through the campus network, The UGC-INFONET Digital Library Consortium additionally provides access to 21 more databases.
